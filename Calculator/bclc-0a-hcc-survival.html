Important Notice

This predictive model was developed and validated using data from patients with BCLC stage 0/A hepatocellular carcinoma and clinically significant portal hypertension. It is intended only to estimate overall survival after curative hepatectomy. The predicted value may differ from actual survival and must not be used as the sole basis for clinical decisions. Treatment plans should be carefully formulated by the treating clinician, integrating the patient's specific condition, preferences, and multidisciplinary assessment. This calculator is provided solely for academic reference, does not constitute medical advice, and carries no legal liability for its predictions.
